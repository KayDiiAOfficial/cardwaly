Privacy Policy

CardWALY does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.

  • All card data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded.
  • We do not use cookies or trackers.
  • We do not access your contacts, location, or other sensitive information.
  • If you use features that require the camera, camera access is only used for barcode scanning and never leaves your device.

If you have any questions about your privacy, contact us at kaydiia.official@gmail.com.
